Everything you need to land your next role

Stop juggling spreadsheets and scattered files. Viumia keeps your entire job search organized in one place.

Track Applications

Log every job you apply to with company, role, status, and salary. Filter by waiting, applied, interview, or rejected.

Document Library

Upload your CVs, cover letters, and certificates once. Link them to any job — no duplicate uploads.

Notes & Comments

Add interview notes, follow-up reminders, and personal comments to every job entry.

Statistics Dashboard

Visualize your job search progress with charts showing applications over time, response rates, and status breakdowns.

100% Offline & Private

Your data stays on your machine. SQLite database, no cloud, no accounts, no tracking. Fully private by design.

Backup & Restore

Export all your data as a ZIP backup and restore it anytime. Your job search is always safe.
